A data science approach to safer streets in London.

This project explores how machine learning, open data, and geospatial analysis can be combined to predict and visualise crime hotspots across the London Metropolitan Police District — supporting smarter, proactive policing decisions.

PURPOSE

Project Overview

This website presents a final year undergraduate data science project focused on predictive policing. It integrates historical crime records, socioeconomic indicators, and spatial data from UK open sources to forecast where crime is most likely to occur across London boroughs.
